# Anthropic invests $100 million into the Claude Partner Network

**Date:** March 12, 2026

## Overview

Anthropic is launching the Claude Partner Network, committing an initial $100 million to support partner organizations helping enterprises adopt Claude. The program provides training, technical support, and joint market development resources.

## Key Components

**Financial Commitment**
- $100 million investment for 2026, with plans for additional funding
- Direct support allocated for training, sales enablement, and market development
- Co-marketing resources for joint campaigns and events

**Partner Support Structure**
- Partner-facing team scaling fivefold
- Dedicated Applied AI engineers for live customer deals
- Technical architects for complex implementations
- Localized go-to-market support in international markets

**Resources & Certifications**
- Partner Portal access with Anthropic Academy training materials
- Sales playbooks from Anthropic's go-to-market team
- Services Partner Directory for enterprise buyer discovery
- Claude Certified Architect, Foundations certification (available now)
- Additional certifications planned for sellers, architects, and developers

**Practical Tools**
- Code Modernization starter kit for legacy codebase migration
- Technical debt remediation support

## Eligibility & Application

Any organization bringing Claude to market is eligible. Membership is free, and applications opened immediately.

## Partner Perspectives

Leading firms including Accenture, Deloitte, Cognizant, and Infosys shared their participation commitments, emphasizing scaling capabilities, governance alignment, and accelerated deployment timelines for enterprise clients.
